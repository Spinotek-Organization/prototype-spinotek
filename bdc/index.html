Common Questions

Do I need to install an app?

No installation needed! Bali DC is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can simply save the dashboard to your home screen for native-like access.

Where can I use the discount?

We have 200+ partners across the most popular areas: Canggu, Seminyak, Uluwatu, and Ubud. Check the explore section in your dashboard for the full list.

Can I share my QR code with friends?

Memberships are personal. The QR code displays your name and is validated by merchants against our live database to prevent fraud.
